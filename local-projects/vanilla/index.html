car puzzle

this is a car puzzle game. the object of the game is to assemble a car from the pieces on the left in less than 30s. you can drag and drop the pieces in the field. if the piece isn't in the right position it will return to the box on the left as tthe last piece. you can click on the hint button to see where the first piece from the box should go but it will cost you 3s. if the timer exceeds 30s you will not be able to drag any piece and you will have lost. to replay the game click in the lost message if you lost or refresh the page in any state.
